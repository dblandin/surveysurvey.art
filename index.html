About

artBangall was a pop-up art exhibition set in the backdrop of a vacant former restaurant in the hamlet of Bangall, NY. A playful riff on today’s contemporary art fairs, the show featured the work of thirty-five emerging artists working in painting, drawing, sculpture, and photography in an unconventional space. artBangall’s intention was to make use of and revitalize a vacant Hudson Valley storefront with a vibrant and diverse art show. The show was curated by two artists, Amanda Brown and Stephanie Del Carpio, both based in New York City.
